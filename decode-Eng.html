About Us

We are FTC teams 28119 & 30222 - Infinite Dream & Navigators from Beijing, China. We are actively preparing for the 2025-2026 season, committed to combining technological innovation with teamwork, striving for excellence in the FIRST Tech Challenge.

Team Composition

We welcome a new team member! Now our team consists of 16 tech and innovation-loving middle school students and 2 supportive instructors, covering programming, mechanical design, electronic engineering, strategy analysis, and other fields. We are building a stronger team for the new season.

Season Goals

For the 2025-2026 season, we aim to achieve breakthrough results in regional and national competitions while expanding our outreach activities to promote STEM education among more young people.

Core Values

Innovation

Continuously explore new technologies and methods, challenge ourselves, and break through the limitations of traditional thinking.

Collaboration

Respect each other's ideas, communicate openly, and work together to achieve common goals through effective teamwork.

Learning

Maintain a humble attitude, keep learning new knowledge and skills, and continuously improve ourselves.

Passion

Maintain enthusiasm for technology and robotics, and inject passion into every task and challenge.
