About Us

We are FTC teams 28119 & 30222 - Infinite Dream & Navigators from Beijing, China. Since our establishment in October 2024, we have been committed to combining technological innovation with teamwork, striving for excellence in the FIRST Tech Challenge.

Team Composition

Our team consists of 15 tech and innovation-loving middle school students and 2 supportive instructors, covering programming, mechanical design, electronic engineering, strategy analysis, and other fields.

Our Goals

We aim to achieve excellent results in competitions while promoting STEM education, inspiring more young people to explore the fascinating world of science and technology.

Team Mission

"Identify problems, explore possibilities, create a better future with technology"

We believe that through STEM education and technological innovation, we can:

Cultivate Critical Thinking

Develop logical and innovative thinking through solving complex problems.

Practice Teamwork

Understand the importance of communication and collaboration to achieve common goals.

Solve Social Problems

Use technological means to focus on and solve practical social issues.

Build Learning Community

Create a positive technology learning community to share knowledge and experience.

Core Values

Following FIRST's core values, we uphold:

Discovery

Curiosity drives us to explore the unknown, continuously learn and grow.

Innovation

Use creativity and imagination to solve challenges and break through traditional thinking.

Impact

Use the power of technology to change the world and create positive social impact.

Inclusion

Embrace different backgrounds and perspectives, creating a diverse and inclusive team environment.

Teamwork

Collaboration achieves extraordinary results; we believe collective wisdom is greater than individual.

Fun

Enjoy the process of exploring technology, maintaining enthusiasm and motivation for learning.
